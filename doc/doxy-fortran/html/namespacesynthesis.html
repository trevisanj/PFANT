Detailed Description

 Fantomol avec sous-programmes (MNP) -
 Calcul possible de 100 angstrom en 100 angstrom.
Note
Flux sortant est en nu: lambda (x-axis) vs. F(nu) (y-axis)
Unit of flux: erg*s^-1*cm^-2/(Hz*ster), however (see next note)
Actually what is called "flux" would be more accurately called "Specific intensity" [Gray Stellar Photospheres 3rd Ed. Eq 5.1]
Flux absolu sortant a ete multiplie par 10**5
Existing files are replaced
